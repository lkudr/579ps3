About Me

I am passionate about the intersection of user experience research and design, library science, and digital accessibility. I approach accessibility holistically, integrating technical accessibility with an understanding of disability culture and community. I love deeply listening to and observing people’s experiences to learn how to make technology better fit their needs. I will receive my Master’s of Science in Information from the University of Michigan in April 2021. In my free time, I love to paint, bike, and travel.
